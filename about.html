About Us

Techastris is a devoted band of innovators, researchers, and environmental enthusiasts working to further marine conservation with techno-based solutions. Under the bigger umbrella of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 14 (Life Below Water), we protect and restore aquatic ecosystems using innovative means. Tools are developed through cooperative efforts to research sustainable practices and create awareness about ocean conservation. Our goal is to directly impact marine biodiversity through pollution reduction, responsible resource management, and a better future for life under water.
